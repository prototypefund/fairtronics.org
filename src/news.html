30.12.2019: Fairtronics interviewed by Deutschlandfunk

While at 36C3, Deutschlandfunk interviewed us for a podcast. We talked about existing social issues with electronics production and possible solutions – and, of course, about the idea behind Fairtronics.

Give it a listen on the Deutschlandfunk website.

27.–30.12.2019: Chaos Communication Congress

Our talk titled Fairtronics was accepted at 36C3. We’re looking forward to meeting you there!

Update: 36C3 was a great success! Thanks to everyone who contacted us, gave feedback, or had discussions with us.

You can watch the recorded presentation on the media.ccc.de site.

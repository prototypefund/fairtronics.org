Nager IT Computer Mouse

Encouraging humane working conditions in the factories of the electronic industries: Nager IT is a Fair-IT pioneer and produces the fairest computer mouse available. Organized as a not-for-profit organization, the project have not only put extensive efforts in their supply chain, exploring sourcing alternatives and working with their suppliers. They are also conducting educational measures on fair production all over Germany.
